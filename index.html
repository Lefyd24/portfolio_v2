About

I hold a BSc in Accounting & Finance and an MSc in Business Analytics from the Athens University of Economics & Business (AUEB), where I graduated second in my class with a GPA of 9.01/10. What drives me is the intersection of data, technology, and business performance—I’m curious about how things work and restless when it comes to learning new tools.

That curiosity pushed me beyond accounting and finance into business intelligence and web development. I now lead business analytics and build enterprise software: internal SaaS platforms (budget, cash flow, attendance, approvals), Power BI dashboards, and client websites. I operate at the intersection of product, data, and engineering—mapping real workflows, designing the right abstractions, and shipping software that teams actually adopt.
